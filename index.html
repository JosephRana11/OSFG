  • Welcome to OSFG Nepal

    OSFG, which stands for Open Source for Good, represents a gathering of young developers and computer science students from different corners of Nepal.

    We all share a deep enthusiasm for coding and using our skills to create a positive social impact. Our mission is to work together to build open source projects that are non profit incentive.

  • Our Mission

    We're aiming to create a platform where developers ,experienced or new join forces to share their ideas and collaborate on social causes. If you're an NGO that could use a hand, feel free to reach out by email with your request.

    Future Plans for OSFG?

    Right now, OSFG is all about coming together to work on exciting projects and building a solid team. Our big dream is to become an official NGO, after we have some meaningfull projects under our belt.

  • A message from the Founder

    "As a developer, I was often contributing to other open source projects. When I started attending in-person local events, I made some really good friends and was amazed by the growing tech community here. This gave me the inspiration to start this initiative as I was involved with other social work activities in the past. I hope we can make a difference together and inspire the next generation of coders."

  • How Projects are Chosen?

    Our process involves the selection of projects through a voting mechanism among our members. Our aim is to make decisions and embark on a fresh project every month, fostering a cooperative and a learning development cycle.

    Even if you're not part of our team, if you have an impactful project idea for a social cause, you're welcome to share it with us via email.

    At the moment we are currently working on our first project , so stay tuned for future updates.

  • How to get involed?

    At the moment we are a small group of developers experimenting with this initiative. Once we have established a comfortable structure and work flow , the gates will be open for any developer or students willing to join us. To recieve further updates , fill out our Form.
